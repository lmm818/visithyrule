Welcome to Hyrule.
Once ruled by goddess Hylia, the Kingdom of Hyrule proudly became her namesake. It boasts a variety of prosperous lands. With lush forests, vast mountain ranges, beautiful fields, amazing small towns, expansive desert, and calming sea, Hyrule has it all.
The Kingdom is also home to the famous Legend of Zelda! Our princess is indeed a remarkable force and we are sure her aura is felt throughout the land.
